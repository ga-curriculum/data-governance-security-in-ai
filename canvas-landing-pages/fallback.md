## About

This lesson covers AI data governance, security, privacy, bias mitigation, transparency, scalability, and Deloitte’s Trustworthy AI Framework, highlighting best practices, challenges, and future trends.

## Content

| Lesson | Est. Delivery Time | Skills |
| ------ |:------------------:| ------ |
| [Introduction to Data Governance in AI](../introduction-to-data-governance-in-ai/README.md) | 10 min | List AI data governance principles, challenges, and emerging trends to ensure ethical, secure, and compliant AI systems.|
| [Data Security in AI](../data-security-in-ai/README.md) | 10 min | Describe AI data security, including threats, encryption, access controls, monitoring, and incident response to protect AI pipelines and ensure data integrity, confidentiality, and compliance.|
| [Privacy Considerations in AI](../privacy-considerations-in-ai/README.md) | 10 min | Describe privacy considerations in AI, including regulatory compliance, anonymization, consent management, privacy-by-design, data protection, and real-world breaches, highlighting best practices and challenges.|
| [Bias in AI and Ethical Considerations](../bias-in-ai-and-ethical-considerations/README.md) | 10 min | Describe bias in AI and ethical considerations, including types, sources, societal impact, detection methods, fairness, diversity, and ethical frameworks to promote responsible AI development.|
| [Data Governance Frameworks for AI](../data-governance-frameworks-for-ai/README.md) | 10 min | Describe AI data governance frameworks, focusing on ownership, quality, tools, and adaptability for secure and compliant AI systems.|
| [Data Lifecycle Management in AI](../data-lifecycle-management-in-ai/README.md) | 10 min | Describe AI data lifecycle management, including collection, preprocessing, storage, access control, versioning, and automation to ensure data security, quality, and compliance.|
| [Transparency and Explainability in AI](../transparency-and-explainability-in-ai/README.md) | 10 min | Describe transparency and explainability in AI, including trust, compliance, model interpretation, communication, privacy balance, and real-world applications, highlighting challenges and best practices.|
| [Future Trends in AI Governance and Security](../future-trends-in-ai-governance-and-security/README.md) | 10 min | Describe AI governance trends, including compliance automation, quantum security, blockchain, IoT, ethics, and future regulations for responsible AI.|
| [Deloitte's Trustworthy AI Framework](../deloittes-trustworthy-ai-framework/README.md) | 10 min | Explain Deloitte’s Trustworthy AI Framework, emphasizing privacy, transparency, fairness, accountability, security, and reliability.|
| **Total content**                                   | 90 min |                                     |
