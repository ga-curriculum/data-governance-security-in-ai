## About
This module explores **AI data governance, security, privacy, and ethical considerations**. Learners will engage in **scenario-based discussions** to apply best practices in real-world contexts, ensuring AI systems are **trustworthy, compliant, and secure**.

## Content

| Lesson | Est. Delivery Time | Skills |
| ------ |:------------------:| ------ |
| [Setup](./setup/README.md) | -- | Set up the development environment. |
| [Data Governance Principles & Frameworks](./microlesson-01/README.md) | 30 min | Understand AI data governance principles, key challenges, and governance frameworks to ensure compliance and security. |
| [Security & Privacy in AI](./microlesson-02/README.md) | 30 min | Identify AI security threats, explore risk mitigation strategies, and evaluate privacy protection methods in AI systems. |
| [AI Ethics, Bias, & Transparency](./microlesson-03/README.md) | 30 min | Examine bias in AI, transparency techniques, and ethical considerations in AI decision-making. Engage in scenario-based discussions to assess fairness and accountability. |
| **Total content** | **90 min** | |
